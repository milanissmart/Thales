How did Thales Find the Height of a pyramid

Thales was also able to find the height of the ancient pyramids. He first put a stick in the ground, then he measured the shadow of the stick. He made a ratio of the length of the stick to the shadow. Lastly, he used that ratio to find the length of the pyramid.

Thales's Theory of Earth

Thales also had a theory that the Earth was flat. He thought that the Earth was a disk floating on water.
